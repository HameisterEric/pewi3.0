The mixed fruit and vegetable land cover in PEWI is based on an equal distribution of four crops: strawberries, grapes, green beans, and squash. Mixed fruit and vegetable land cover in PEWI assumes effective management practices as noted by Taber (2009).

Mixed fruit and vegetable land-use assumes two phosphorus management methods in equal proportion: surface application with no incorporation and incorporation within one week (Matthew Helmers and Thomas Isenhart, Iowa State University, personal communication, 2014; Chennault 51).
