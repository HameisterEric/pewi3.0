The General equation is: $$\sum_{i=1}^n \sum_{j=1}^{m_i} A_{ij} * YB_{ij}[YieldType] * YP_{ij}$$ where \(A_{ij}\) is the area of the grid cell, \(YB_{ij}[Yield Type]\) is the yield base rate for the type of product, and \(YP_{ij}\) is the yield precipitation factor.
